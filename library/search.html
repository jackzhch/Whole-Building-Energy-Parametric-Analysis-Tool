Library

Building materials specification database

You can use this section of the WBEPT to find completely detailed specification sheets for any building material you need. Use the tabs on top to select the level of your building that you're working with, and use the sidebar on the left to navigate through different material categories in that level.

You can also use the button to the right to add new material specification sheets to add new materials to our library. We certainly appreciate your contribution!
